<objective>
Make the dashboard slider preview line render at full per-sample resolution
for small / freshly-live sensor datasets (numel(x) <= 100), and only start
downsampling once more than 100 samples are available.

Purpose: Users see stair-stepped, coarse preview lines during the first
minutes of live capture because `floor(numel(x)/2)` halves the bucket count
even for tiny datasets. The user wants raw fidelity until ~100 samples,
then graceful downsampling thereafter.

Output: Two-line behavioral change in `FastSenseWidget.getPreviewSeries`
(applied at both nBucketsEff sites — initial computation and post-NaN-drop
recomputation), gated by a private class constant `PreviewRawThreshold_ = 100`,
plus regression tests pinning the boundary.
</objective>
